Selected Projects

Zero Emission Gas Well

Spring 2021-Fall 2021

The project was sponsored by Nadal & Gussman, a natural gas company located in Ruston, LA. The company desired a solution to reduce natural gas emissions at well sites. During operation of the pneumatic devices, natural gas is emitted to the environment. The major constituent of natural gas is methane, which is a greenhouse gas hazardous than carbon dioxide. The engineering team successfully fabricated a solution which included a storage thank that supplied compressed air to operate the devices, instead of the natural gas previously used. This design completely eliminates the emissions from pneumatic device operation. A remote area power supply (RAPS) system was implemented into the design, as the locations of the gas well-sites do not have access to the power grid.
